Mental Health Support & Wellness

You are not alone. Your feelings are valid.

Understanding Mental Health

Mental health is just as important as physical health. It affects how we think, feel, and act. Everyone faces emotional challenges, but support and coping strategies can help you manage them.

Common Areas of Struggle

  • Anxiety and overthinking
  • Depression or persistent sadness
  • Stress and burnout
  • Loneliness and emotional pain
  • Lack of motivation or direction

What You Can Do

You can start with simple steps:

  • Talk to someone you trust
  • Practice slow deep breathing
  • Write down your thoughts
  • Go for a short walk
  • Get enough sleep

Helpful Resources

These platforms provide support and guidance:

  • 7 Cups: Free emotional support chat
  • YourDOST: Online counseling (India)
  • MentalHealth.gov

If You Need Immediate Help

If you are feeling unsafe or in crisis, please reach out to a professional or emergency helpline near you. You deserve help and support.
