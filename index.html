Mental Health Awareness

Your Mental Health Matters

Good mental health improves how we think, feel, act, and connect with others.

Learn more Get Help
Tools, tips & emotional support in one place.

Why Mental Health Matters

  • Improves well-being
  • Builds resilience
  • Boosts productivity
  • Strengthens relationships
  • Supports personal growth

Common Issues

  • Stress
  • Anxiety
  • Depression
  • Burnout
  • Loneliness
  • Sleep problems

Warning Signs

  • Feeling sad or empty
  • Isolation
  • Loss of interest
  • Low energy
  • Changes in sleep
  • Difficulty focusing

Self Care

  • Sleep well
  • Eat healthy
  • Move daily
  • Practice mindfulness
  • Talk to someone

Helping a Friend

  • Listen carefully
  • Support without judging
  • Encourage professional help
  • Check in regularly
