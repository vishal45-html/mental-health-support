Mental Health Awareness – Dark Neon Website

1. What Is Mental Health?

Mental health affects how we think, feel and act. It is important for emotional well-being, decision-making, and relationships.

2. Stress & Anxiety

Stress comes from academic pressure, deadlines and personal expectations. Anxiety comes from overthinking and worrying excessively.

3. Depression & Overthinking

Depression affects mood, sleep, appetite, and interest in daily activities. Overthinking makes the mind tired and stressed.

4. Self-care & Healing

Healing happens slowly. Meditation, sleep, exercise, and talking to someone you trust helps your mental stability.
