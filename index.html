Mental Health Awareness – Dark Neon Glow

1. What is Mental Health?

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act.

2. Stress & Anxiety

Stress and anxiety are common among students, mainly due to workload, overthinking, and expectations.

3. Self Care & Meditation

Regular self-care such as meditation, rest, exercise, and healthy eating improves mental well-being.

4. Helping a Friend

Listen, support, and encourage your friend to talk about their feelings. Being there matters.
