Mental Health Awareness

Your mind matters. Mental health affects how we think, feel, and act. Understanding it helps us build strength, balance, and peace.

Explore More
Calm your mind. Understand your emotions. Grow stronger.

Why Mental Health Matters

Well‑being, emotional balance, and clarity in daily life.
Resilience — handling stress and bounce‑back ability.
Stronger relationships and better communication.
Higher productivity and academic focus.

Common Issues

Stress, anxiety & burnout
Depression & emotional overload
Sleep disturbance & isolation
Overthinking & low confidence

Warning Signs

Persistent sadness or mood changes
Loss of interest in daily activities
Sleep & appetite changes
Feeling hopeless or drained

Self Care Tips

Proper sleep & hydration
Mindfulness & breathing exercises
Regular movement / exercise
Positive self‑talk & gratitude

How to Help a Friend

Listen without judgement
Encourage opening up
Stay connected — small check‑ins
Suggest professional help when needed

Myths vs Facts

MythFact
