About Mental Health

Mental health includes emotional, psychological, and social well-being. It affects how we think, feel, and act. This website provides tips, warning signs, and support resources for everyone.

Common Signs to Watch For

Mood changes

Persistent sadness, irritability, or mood swings.

Withdrawal

Avoiding friends, family, and activities.

Sleep changes

Insomnia or oversleeping.

Concentration

Difficulty focusing or making decisions.

Resources

Hotlines

  • Example: 123-456-7890
  • Text: HELP to 80000

Self-Help

Try breathing exercises, journaling, and grounding.

  1. 4-4-4 breathing
  2. 10-minute walk
  3. Write one sentence about your feeling

Professional Help

Consult therapists, counsellors, or psychiatrists for support.

Need immediate help?

If you or someone else is at risk of self-harm, contact emergency services immediately.

FAQ

How do I know if I need help?

If changes in mood, sleep, energy, or thinking last more than two weeks, consider seeking support.

What can I do for a friend?

Listen without judgment, ask if they're safe, and encourage professional support.
